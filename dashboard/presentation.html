The Problem with Keyword Filters

The job description contains an intentional trap for naive matching systems.

“The right answer is not ‘find candidates whose skills section contains the most AI keywords.’ That’s a trap we’ve explicitly built into the dataset.”

— From the Job Description, Senior AI Engineer (Founding Team)
  • 01 Candidates list 20+ AI skills with 0 months of actual usage
  • 02 Non-technical profiles stuffed with expert-level ML keywords
  • 03 Consulting-only careers with no production engineering evidence
  • 04 Impossible tenure durations at known startups
  • 05 Pure research profiles without deployment experience

Three-Signal Scoring

Each signal targets a different failure mode. Weights reflect what matters for a founding-team hire: conceptual depth and proven delivery, not just availability.

SEMANTIC
40%
LSA cosine similarity between JD and candidate narrative embeddings
STRUCTURAL
40%
Title match, experience band (6-8yr ideal), duration-weighted skills, career evidence
BEHAVIORAL
20%
Response rate, recency, notice period, GitHub activity, open-to-work

Two-Phase Architecture

Phase 1 · Runs Once

Pre-Computation

~1.8 min
  • Loads 100K candidate profiles from JSONL
  • Builds narrative documents from summaries, career history, and duration-filtered skills
  • TF-IDF vectorization: 30K features (unigrams + bigrams)
  • Truncated SVD: compresses to 384 latent semantic dimensions
  • Saves embeddings + fitted models to disk
Phase 2 · Ranking Engine

Score, Filter, Rank

~5.1 sec
Step 1
Transform JD text through the same TF-IDF + SVD pipeline
Step 2
Compute cosine similarity against all 100K candidate vectors
Step 3
Apply structural scoring to top 3K semantic candidates
Step 4
Run honeypot detection, penalties, and generate reasoning
04 / 06

Penalties & Honeypot Detection

Hard filters that enforce the JD's explicit disqualifiers. Multipliers are not decorative — they reflect severity.

Honeypot Filter ×0.01

  • 10+ expert AI skills but 0 months career experience in any
  • Non-technical titles (e.g. Marketing Manager) with deep ML skill lists
  • Skills that completely contradict career history descriptions
  • Impossibly long tenures at known early-stage startups

Consulting-Only ×0.10

  • Entire career at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini
  • JD explicitly warns: "We've had bad fit experiences"
  • Mixed careers (consulting + product) are not penalized

Domain Mismatch ×0.15

  • Primary expertise in CV/speech/robotics without NLP or IR experience
  • Non-technical title with no ML career history at all

Experience Bounds ×0.08

  • Less than 2 years total experience — too junior for founding role
  • Score tapering applied outside the 5–9 year ideal band
final_score = ( semantic × 0.40 + structural × 0.40 + behavioral × 0.20 ) × disqualifier_penalty × honeypot_penalty
